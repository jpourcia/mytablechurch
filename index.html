Welcome

A place to learn Christ and walk with His people

We believe church is not mainly a building or a stage. The New Testament shows believers gathering as a people called out by Christ, devoted to the apostles’ teaching, fellowship, breaking of bread, and prayers.

Whether you are new to the faith, returning to Scripture, or looking for a more personal and biblical setting for worship and discipleship, you are welcome here.

Our vision

What we mean by “Ekklesia”

Called-out people

The ekklesia is the assembly of those who belong to Jesus Christ. It is a people, not merely a place.

Life together

We gather in homes, around tables, in prayer, in Scripture, and in mutual care. Every believer matters. Every member can grow and serve.

Centered on Christ

Our aim is not performance, ritual, or image. Our aim is to know Christ, obey Him, and help others do the same.
